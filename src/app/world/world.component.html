Sean's World Map

Welcome to an interactable map of the world. Feel free to scroll around the map in all directions. You'll discover information about the countries on planet Earth. Clicking on a country will display the Name, Capital, Region, Income Level, Lending Type, and Coordinates.

Please be patient with your clicks! Our development team has found quick repeated calls to the API can bog down our system. Happy learning!
